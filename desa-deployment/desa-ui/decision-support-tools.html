SANSA Decision Support System

The SANSA Decision Support System provides Earth Observation-based tools that support planning, monitoring, sustainability, and evidence-based decision-making across key national priority areas.

DESA serves as a central information and access portal, while the SANSA Decision Support Tools platform provides operational decision-support tools for users who need applied Earth Observation products and services.

Decision Support Domains

The platform supports five key domains where satellite data can contribute to improved monitoring, response, planning, and resource management.
